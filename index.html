Welcome to my site!

Hi, this is Xiyun Li (Chinese: 李曦云) from Yangquan, China. I am currently a first-year PhD student in Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My supervisors are Prof. Xu Bo, and Prof. Xu jiaming.

Research Interests

Broadly speaking, my research interests include speech/audio processing, speech enhancement/separation (e.g., speech denoising and dereverberation), deep learning and human speech perception under adverse acoustic conditions.
